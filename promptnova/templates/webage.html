How It Works

User sends a prompt, Prompt Nova analyzes it using rules, ML, and policies. A hybrid risk score (0–100) is generated to decide whether to Allow, Sanitize, or Block.

Hybrid Technology

Three-layer defense: Rule-Based Detection, ML Semantic Analysis (TF-IDF + Logistic Regression), and a Policy Engine. Balances accuracy and speed.
